VENTURE CITY HACKATHON

The joy meeting an expert teacher, for the craving learner. It is a web application that connects people in 1 km vicinity matching those who can teach with those who want to Learn.

Corporate Philosophy
  • Used to do things that were not possible in the past.
  • To provide student and teacher satisfaction in financial and humanistic terms.
  • Applied to things we already do
  • an inquiry-based learning activity that directs learners in using information from the web.
